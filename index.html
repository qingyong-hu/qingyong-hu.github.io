About

Hi! I am a Post-doctoral Research Fellow in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) under the guidance of Prof. Qian Zhang, where I received my PhD degree in 2025 August. I obtained my bachelor's degree from the School of the Gift Young at University of Science and Technology (USTC), majoring in Computer Science and advised by Prof. Wei Gong.

My research lies at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence of Things (AIoT), ubiquitous sensing, and deep learning, with the goal of building efficient, robust, and intelligent systems for smart healthcare and wireless applications. I have recently received the ACM IMWUT Distinguished Paper Award (2025) for our work on contactless arterial blood pressure waveform monitoring.

My research agenda currently focuses on three thrusts:

  • AIoT for Humam-centric Sensing
  • AI for IoT System Optimization
  • Robust and Scalable Machine Learning
